Emergency preparedness

Learn about emergency procedures and preparation, getting emergency alerts, becoming a first responder, severe weather procedures, and obtaining first-aid supplies.

Prevention

Read our tips and procedures for fire safety, pedestrian and cyclist safety, and safety while travelling. Learn the correct procedures for hot work and access fire prevention forms.

Campus safety: be informed

In case of an emergency on campus, call: 514-848-3717 (extension 3717 from an internal phone).

With 2 campuses and over 70 buildings, Security dispatchers can connect you with 911 operators quickly, while coordinating the repsonse and saving valuable time.
