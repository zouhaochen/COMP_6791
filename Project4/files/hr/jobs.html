Current job opportunities

Engage with big questions. Search for solutions and make positive contributions to society. Concordia creativity powers Montreal and connects us to the world. Work to redefine the 21st century university.

Employer of choice

The university receives recognition for its response to the COVID-19 pandemic.


Why work at Concordia?

With more than 7,000 faculty and staff, Concordia enables individuals to grow, contribute and innovate, whatever their careers. The university offers:

  • A highly diverse community in one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the world.
  • A leading-edge teaching and research environment.
  • Competitive compensation and benefits, opportunities to learn and develop — and more.

Campus life

Not only does Concordia have a unique, vibrant and rich campus life, the university also enjoys the benefit of having two campuses that are distinct and diverse. From state-of-the-art facilities for research and experimentation, to renovated office spaces and a leafy campus quad, there is a place for everyone.

Discover more about life in Montreal


Community involvement

Public engagement and sustainability are deeply rooted in the Concordia identity. Faculty, staff and students are staying connected with the community and making a difference. There are many ways to stay informed and get involved at Concordia.
