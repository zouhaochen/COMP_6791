BIRKS STUDENT SERVICE CENTRE

Your gateway to Concordia

We are available to assist you remotely or in person. Drop by Monday to Friday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Call us from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. at 514-848-2424, ext. 2668. Or, email us at students@concordia.ca.

Consult the list of services offered

How we can help

If you have a block on your account, questions about a university policy or procedure, are unsure where to turn for your situation, or have any other questions, start with us. We’ll help you to understand the matter and make a plan to resolve it.

Location

Room LB-185
J.W. McConnell Building
1400 De Maisonneuve Blvd. W.

Reach us

Tel: 514-848-2424, ext. 2668
Fax: 514-848-4507
Email: students@concordia.ca

Hours of operation

In person service:
Monday to Friday
10 a.m. – 4 p.m.

By phone:
Monday to Friday
9 a.m. – 5 p.m.
